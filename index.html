Meet Louise

I've Dedicated My Life
to This Work

"I started in this profession because I believe that every person deserves a farewell that truly honours who they were — and every family deserves someone they can genuinely trust."

I'm Louise Walch, an independent funeral director based in Droylsden, Manchester. I founded Louise Walch Family Funeral Services to offer something the large corporate funeral chains simply cannot — a deeply personal, one-to-one service rooted in genuine care.

Over 24 years, I've guided hundreds of families through some of the hardest moments of their lives. I know how overwhelming it can feel, and I know that what families need most is someone calm, experienced, and completely honest. That is what I bring to every arrangement I make.
